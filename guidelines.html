THE BRAND

The basics

The OnBrand brand was created by a team of enthousiastic and creative people within Bynder.
It started with a simple idea: let's bring marketing and creative professionals together to discuss the trends set to shape the future of branding. Five years on, we’ve grown to become Europe’s leading branding conference.

The logo

Our logo is based on simple geometric shapes. It is carefully constructed to maintain ownable characteristics while allowing for perfect legibility at any size on any application. The underscored O from OnBrand becomes the logomark when used on it’s own.

The woodmark

The OnBrand woodmark is used on any external facing commuication. The clear space around the woodmark is equal to the cap height of the logomark (underscored O).

The logomark

The logomark is used on our social media channels, as an icon and in case we need to place our logo in a limited space. This mark can stand alone, as long as the core logo is somewhere else in the finished piece. In any other case we use the woodmark.

Typography

The main purpose of our typography is readability and personality. We use two typefaces across all our commuinication - Montserrat and PT Serif. Montserrat is used for display-type treatment where our message needs to be loud and confident. PT Serif is used mostly for body text because of its legibility and ease of use.

Pairings

It is important to maintain the following type pairing to allow for clarity, consistenct and a strong hierarchy for all communications. Medium weight should be paired with Light and Bold with Regular weight.

Calls to action
