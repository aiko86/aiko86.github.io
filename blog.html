The social media wrap-up of #OnBrand18

Europe’s leading branding conference is sadly over for 2018, but it will live on forever through social media (just like #OnBrand17). With 4 different stages, close to 50 speakers, and plenty going on around the edges—interactive workshops, free coffee and VR experiences, food trucks, the Bynder Bar and more—it was impossible to be everywhere at once and take everything in.

Thank goodness for hashtags, right?! As well as catching up on what we missed, we loved seeing #OnBrand18 through your eyes—so keep those selfies, pearls of wisdom, quotes and Dad jokes coming.
